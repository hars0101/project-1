Project Title:

Innovative Aerial Surveillance for Crime Prevention in Urban Environments.

Problem Statement:

Urban areas face increasing challenges with crime, especially in densely populated regions. Traditional security systems often lack coverage or fail to detect potential threats in real-time. There is a pressing need for an intelligent system capable of providing 24/7 surveillance to identify criminal activity and prevent incidents before they escalate.

Proposed Solution:

We propose a cutting-edge aerial surveillance solution that leverages drone technology equipped with real-time facial recognition and behavioral analysis. This system will autonomously patrol urban areas, identifying suspicious individuals or groups based on predefined patterns. Once an anomaly is detected, the system will instantly alert the relevant authorities with critical information, such as location, time, and potential threat level. By combining advanced AI, drones, and machine learning, this solution offers a proactive approach to urban safety and crime prevention.

Title:

Smart Waste Management: Autonomous Drones for Waste Detection and Reporting

Problem Statements:

Current waste management systems face inefficiencies due to overflowing bins, missed pickups, and high costs. These issues impede sustainability goals, disrupt recycling programs, and contribute to environmental pollution. The need for real-time monitoring and better waste management practices has never been more urgent.

The solution involves autonomous drones equipped with high-resolution cameras and ultrasonic sensors. Title:

3D Landslide Mapping and Rescue Support System Using Drones

Problem Statements:

Landslides cause massive damage to lives, property, and the environment. After a landslide, rescuing people becomes difficult Because: • There is no real-time data about the terrain. • Finding safe paths for rescue teams takes too much time. • Manual surveys are risky and slow. The Impact: • People lose their lives and homes. • Roads and communication systems are disrupted. • The environment is severely affected.

Solution Description:

To address the challenges of landslide rescue operations, we propose using drones equipped with advanced sensors and AI technology: Pre-Landslide: Capture and Store Data: Drones will capture and store terrain data to create a detailed baseline map. Post-Landslide: Gather New Data: Drones will collect new terrain data to generate updated maps. Analysis: Advanced software will compare old and new maps to identify safe and unsafe areas. Communication: These findings will be shared with rescue teams in real- time, speeding up rescue operations. This solution provides accurate, realtime information on safe routes and hazardous zones, enhancing the efficiency and safety of rescue efforts.
